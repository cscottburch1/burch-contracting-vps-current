Executive Summary
This comprehensive GEO audit analyzed 50 pages across burchcontracting.com, evaluating the
site's readiness for AI-driven search and citation. With an overall GEO score of 69/100, Burch
Contracting currently sits in the 'Fair' category—indicating a moderate foundation with

Methodology
This GEO audit employs a multi-dimensional scoring framework evaluating: Citability (answer
block quality, self-containment, statistical density, uniqueness), Brand Authority (presence on
knowledge platforms used by LLMs for veriﬁcation), E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise,
Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness signals), Technical Infrastructure (crawler access, site
