# Burch Contracting Short-Form Video Script

## Primary Version (20-25 seconds)

### Format
- Length: 20-25 seconds
- Style: Fast-paced, clean, professional, trustworthy
- Music: Uplifting modern instrumental with a light beat drop at the transformation moment
- Visual style: Modern construction visuals, quick cuts, bold captions, local feel

### Shot-by-shot Script

Visual:

- Quick zoom and hard cut between old finishes, poor lighting, worn cabinets

- STILL LOOKING OUTDATED?

Caption:
- This is why your bathroom still looks outdated.

## Editor Notes
- Keep cuts between 0.5 and 1.5 seconds for the first 10 seconds.
- Use bold all-caps overlays for key pain points and service categories.
- Add subtle whoosh transitions into the transformation sequence.
- Let the music lift at the first finished-space reveal.
- Keep captions centered low, with high contrast for mobile viewing.
- End frame should hold phone number and website for at least 2 seconds.

## Recommended Caption Styling
- Font style: Bold sans serif
- Text treatment: White text with dark shadow or dark translucent background
- Emphasis words: OUTDATED, TRANSFORM, FREE ESTIMATE
